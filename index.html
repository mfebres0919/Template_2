APPOINTMENTS

All appointments must be booked in advance through the booking system. Please select the correct service when booking to ensure enough time is reserved. If you are unsure which service to choose, feel free to reach out before booking.

DEPOSIT

A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. The deposit goes toward your total service price and confirms your time slot. Appointments are not guaranteed without a deposit.

LATE ARRIVALS

Please arrive on time for your appointment. Clients arriving more than 10–15 minutes late may need to reschedule or have their service adjusted to avoid impacting other clients. Late arrivals may still be charged the full service price.

CANCELLATIONS

Cancellations or reschedules must be made at least 24 hours in advance. Failure to do so may result in the loss of your deposit. No-shows will be charged accordingly and may require full payment before rebooking.
